Cancel a running child (and recursively all of its descendants).

You may only cancel agents in your own subtree. The cancellation is asynchronous: the target receives a `cancel` message, aborts its current LLM turn, marks itself `canceled`, and posts a `child_failed{reason:"canceled"}` back to its parent.

Parameters:
- agent_id: the agent to cancel
- reason: optional short explanation

Use this when a child is going off-track, has become redundant, or you want to stop a whole branch to conserve budget.
