Every paper or article referenced in the report MUST include a brief introduction covering: what problem it addresses, its core method/insight, and its relevance to the research topic. Do NOT cite a paper by title alone — always accompany it with a 2-4 sentence introduction.

- When referencing a paper, article, or any published work, the title MUST be wrapped in 《》 and the title itself MUST be a clickable link to the source URL: `[《Paper Title》](URL)`. The link points directly to the paper page (arXiv abstract, open-access PDF, or official publisher page). Include a brief 2-4 sentence introduction after the link explaining the paper. Example: `[《Attention Is All You Need》](https://arxiv.org/abs/1706.03762) — 介绍了Transformer架构，完全基于注意力机制取代循环和卷积，在机器翻译任务上达到SOTA性能，是本研究的核心参考方法。`

- Prefer open-access URLs (arXiv, open-access journals, institutional repositories). Avoid paywalled URLs when an open-access alternative exists.
